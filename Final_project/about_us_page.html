About OWL MIND

OWL MIND is an AI education platform aimed at service companies. After spending 6 months at KPMG working in strategy consulting and talking with many colleagues there, I realized that there is a massive knowledge gap in the AI world. Every employee wants to be able to use it better, and managers expect their employees to leverage it, but the real uses are still extremely superficial.

In big service companies, change happens slowly, and reskilling of employees does too. OWL MIND wants to prevent employees from getting left behind in this fast-changing environment by creating a platform that helps empower employees to reach their full potential with AI.

AI solutions don't have a one-size-fits-all - OWL MIND believes that even though developing a killer app could be useful in the end, every person needs to use AI differently and for different very specific tasks; therefore, giving employees the tools to fish instead of the fish itself is the ultimate goal.

The name OWL MIND represents our vision of empowering professionals with a second mind through strategic AI adoption. The name symbolizes the wisdom and curiosity that owls naturally embody—qualities essential for navigating the evolving AI landscape. Just as owls are known for their perceptive vision and adaptability, our platform helps employees see beyond conventional workflows and adapt to new technologies. This name also carries personal significance, connecting back to scouting experiences where the owl served as a spirit animal, representing the same thoughtful approach and natural inquisitiveness we aim to foster in the professional services community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help employees learn about, leverage, and use AI safely in their current jobs to prevent becoming redundant while helping medium to large-sized service companies achieve increased efficiency.
